Cappuccino Recipe

Cappuccino is a popular coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. Here’s how to make the perfect cappuccino at home.

  1. Prepare a shot of espresso.
  2. Steam milk until frothy.
  3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso.
  4. Top with milk foam.
  5. Optionally, sprinkle cocoa or cinnamon on top.

Iced Coffee Recipe

Iced coffee is perfect for hot days when you want a refreshing and cool coffee drink. Here’s a simple recipe to make iced coffee at home.

  1. Brew a strong pot of coffee and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour the cooled coffee over the ice.
  4. Add milk, cream, or sweeteners as desired.
  5. Stir and enjoy your iced coffee!

Mocha Recipe

Mocha is a delicious mix of coffee and chocolate, perfect for when you want something sweet and caffeinated. Here’s how to make it.

  1. Brew a shot of espresso or strong coffee.
  2. Mix 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder and sugar into the coffee.
  3. Steam milk and pour it over the coffee-cocoa mixture.
  4. Top with whipped cream and drizzle chocolate syrup.
